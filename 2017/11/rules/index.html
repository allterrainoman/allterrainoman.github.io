Rules

November 11, 2017 in Main Pages, Info

These rules are not intended to restrict your fun in the use of your four wheel drive vehicle. Rather, they are reminders and ideas we have found which will increase your enjoyment of the greatest of all outdoor sports, while providing safety for you and your family. - All new members must attend a meet and greet barbecue day that introduces them to the safe recovery practises that All-Terrain Adventures Oman uses as well as the “nuts and bolts” of how we run and organise our trips. For some members the meet and greet is the first introduction to four-wheel driving, so each individual vehicle’s capabilities and equipment are discussed and checked with suggestions made for improvements or additions that are needed. Refresher events are also compulsory for all members after a set period of time (1 year) so that everyone can keep up to date with the latest techniques and safe practices involved with vehicle recovery.

  • Safety is of the utmost importance. Safety on the trail is above pride at all times.

  • Safety equipment is imperative. Equipment such as a basic first aid kit and fire extinguisher are required in all vehicles. Tow hooks, straps, and so forth are highly recommended and will be required on some trails. Refer to the Equipment page on the website.

  • While driving off-road, there is always an opportunity for Mother Nature to reach out and touch you. Therefore, any damage that occurs to your vehicle while on a club trip is your personal responsibility. Not that of anyone affiliated with All-Terrain Adventures Oman.

  • Obey Oman rules, laws and regulations. Driving off designated roads and trails will not be allowed or tolerated.

  • Show good sportsmanship on and off the trail.

  • Always keep vehicles ahead and behind you in sight at all times while on trips or outings. We leave no Member behind. When out of sight of the vehicle behind you, stop and flash your headlights to the vehicle ahead.

  • Any items pertaining to the club such as trips, events or outside activities will be reviewed discussed and/or voted on before pursuing the plans. This will happen at regular meet and greet events.

  • Members work as a team and help each other out when in need.

  • Respect each others opinions and thoughts, and keep open minds.

  • Pets, when brought on a trip, MUST BE KEPT ON A LEASH at all times.

  • Matches, smokes and campfires must be carefully watched and snuffed out properly.

  • The inappropriate use of alcohol is refrained during the use of a vehicle and the responsibility relies solely on the user. There will not be any alcoholic beverages allowed on day trips. During overnight trips at camp, discretionary use will be allowed.

  • We are a “leave no trace” group. Don’t throw cans, paper or other litter from your vehicle. When camping, make sure that no rubbish is left behind, not even a cigarette stub.

You will be required to sign a document agreeing to the above rules before you will be allowed to accompany All-Terrain Adventures Oman on any trips.
